Data Handling

Do We Sell Your Information?

Third Parties

Our primary source of revenue is advertisements, and we use Google AdMob for this purpose. Google AdMob collects certain information, as outlined in their privacy policy here. We ensure that any third party we share user data with provides equal protection as stated in this privacy policy.

All user data collected by Google AdMob is encrypted in transit using the Transport Layer Security (TLS) protocol.

By default, Google AdMob collects information about:

  • Your device’s IP address - Which may be used to estimate the general location of a device
  • User product interactions - Collects user product interactions and interaction information, including app launch, taps, and video views.
  • Diagnostic information - Collects information related to the performance of your app and the SDK, including crash logs, app launch time, hang rate, and energy usage.
  • Device and Account identifiers - Collects Android advertising (ad) ID, app set ID, and, if applicable, other identifiers related to signed-in accounts on the device.

While Google AdMob collects information, we do not store any of this information on our servers.

We also utilize Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google, Inc. ("Google"). Google Analytics uses cookies, which are text files placed on your device, to help us analyze how users interact with our mobile application. The information generated by the cookie about your use of the mobile application (including your IP address) will be transmitted to and stored by Google on servers.

The information collected through Google Analytics is used to evaluate user interactions with our mobile application, compile reports on application activity, and provide other services related to application usage and internet usage. This data helps us improve our mobile application, tailor content to user preferences, and enhance overall user experience.

Data Storage

Google Firebase encrypts data in transit and encrypts data at rest.
